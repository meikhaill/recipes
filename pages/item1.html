Baked Bean Morning Delight!

This meal is best eaten soon after you awaken from your deep 2 hour long slumber

Requirements include

  • Can of your favorite baked beans! (refried works as well.)
  • A bowl! but if you're eating this you probably cant afford a bowl so your hands work too!
  • Milk! Expired milk will add an added kick to each bite!(optional)

This is a very simple recipe that will have you up and ready to go in no time.

NOTE!!! Cook time: 4-5 minutes

Instructions

  1. Open your favorite can of baked beans, brand name will not matter here, but make sure you give it a good mix before doing anything else. Give the beans time to rest.
  2. Grab your milk out of the fridge as well as a bowl. This will be the most important step as we would just simply be eating normal beans without. NOT OPTIONAL
  3. Lastly, combine both beans and milk, stir thoroughly and let sit for 1-2 minutes. Enjoy!
